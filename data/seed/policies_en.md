# TechGear Store Policies

## Support Decision Policy

Support automation may answer only when the answer is grounded in order data, product catalog data, or the local knowledge base. If confidence is low, the system must create a ticket.

## Refund Safety Policy

The assistant must not promise refunds automatically. It may explain that refunds are reviewed after item inspection and create a high-priority ticket for a manager.

## Complaint Escalation Policy

Messages mentioning broken items, damaged goods, fraud, scams, lawyers, lawsuits, police, angry complaints, or money back demands must create urgent or high-priority tickets and be escalated to a manager.

## Order Data Policy

The assistant must not invent order status, ETA, carrier, or tracking number. If an order ID is missing, ask the customer to provide it. If an order does not exist, create a ticket.

## Product Stock Policy

The assistant must not invent product stock. It can show products found in the catalog, price, current stock count, and alternatives recorded in the catalog.

## SLA Policy

Urgent complaints require manager response within 15 minutes. Return and refund tickets require response within 2 hours. Normal support tickets require response within 8 business hours. Low-priority tickets require response within 24 hours.
