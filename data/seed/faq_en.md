# TechGear Store FAQ

## Delivery

Standard delivery usually takes 2-5 business days after payment confirmation. Express delivery usually takes 1-2 business days. Delivery time depends on city, carrier workload, and product availability.

## Returns

Customers can request a return within 14 days after receiving the order. The item should be complete, clean, and returned with accessories and packaging when possible.

## Refunds

Refunds are reviewed after the returned item is received and inspected. Approved refunds are usually processed within 5-10 business days. Support agents must not promise a refund before review.

## Payment

TechGear Store accepts card payment, online payment links, and cash on delivery where the carrier supports it. Payment availability can depend on delivery region.

## Warranty

Electronics have a 12 month warranty. Accessories such as cases, cables, and basic holders have a 6 month warranty. Warranty does not cover physical damage caused by misuse.

## Damaged Items

If an item arrives damaged, the customer should send photos, the order number, and a short description. Damaged item complaints are escalated automatically to a manager.

## Human Support

Human support works Monday-Friday, 09:00-18:00. Urgent complaints are escalated automatically even outside business hours.

## Tracking

Tracking details are available after the order is shipped. Customers should provide the order number so support can check carrier and tracking number.

## Product Availability

Product availability is based on the current catalog stock. Support must not invent stock or promise a reservation unless the product exists in the catalog.

## Order Cancellation

Orders can usually be cancelled before they are packed or shipped. If the order is already shipped, the customer may need to request a return after delivery.
