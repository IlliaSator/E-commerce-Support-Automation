# Commercial Use Case

Target customer: a small e-commerce store selling electronics and accessories.

Pain:

- repetitive order, delivery, payment, warranty, and stock questions
- slow manual order checks
- missed complaints and refund risk
- no SLA visibility
- limited support reporting

Value:

- faster safe answers
- automatic complaint escalation
- SLA control
- manager reports
- AI suggestion review data

Automated:

- order lookup
- FAQ/policy answers
- product availability search
- ticket creation
- escalation notifications
- SLA breach reporting
- daily and weekly summaries

Human review remains required for refunds, complaints, legal threats, low-confidence answers, and customer requests for a manager.

Positioning: this is a production-style MVP and commercial automation foundation, not a fully production-ready SaaS.
